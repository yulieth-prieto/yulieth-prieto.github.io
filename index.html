Yulieth Prieto-M

«Since September 2024, I have been an Assistant Professor at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile. I obtained my PhD in Algebraic Geometry in March 2022 from the University of Bologna. From 2022 to 2024, I held a postdoctoral position at the International Centre for Theoretical Physics (ICTP) in Trieste, under the supervision of Lothar Göttsche.»

I specialize in Complex Algebraic Geometry, with a primary focus on Hyperkähler manifolds and moduli spaces. My research interests also extend to birational geometry, particularly exploring special connections of Fano varieties with Hyperkähler manifolds, as well as the study of automorphism groups and derived categories. I am also interested in the moduli of vector bundles on projective manifolds.

Currently, I am partially supported by the INICIO-2025-13 grant, titled "Nuevas direcciones en las variedades hyperkähler y su interacción con las variedades de Fano" ID 250623055.
